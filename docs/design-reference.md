# Design Reference — Higher Drones

## Design objective

Build a fast, static, GitHub Pages-compatible website that positions Higher Drones as a premium drone videography marketplace.

The site should use `design/mockups/primary-mockup.png` as visual inspiration for cinematic scale, altitude cues, premium service cards, pilot profile cards, and gallery-forward storytelling. Do not copy risky mockup mechanics such as secure booking, payments, licensing, certification, training, drone shows, or operational approval claims.

## Visual direction

- Premium, cinematic, visual, trustworthy, modern, easy to book, accessible, responsive, and pilot-friendly.
- Use sky blue, deep teal, white cards, subtle altitude/grid cues, and polished gallery panels.
- Keep the hero contained on desktop and mobile.
- Make the primary CTAs visible without creating a payment or booking backend.
- Use cards for services, pilot profiles, gallery categories, resources, and planning steps.

## Safety rules

Do not add:

- Forms, payments, subscriptions, commissions, affiliate plans, merchandise revenue, data services, dashboards, account systems, analytics, tracking pixels, APIs, backend logic, or dependencies.
- Automatic booking, automatic pilot matching, customer uploads, calendar scheduling, payment collection, or marketplace transaction mechanics.
- Regulatory, airspace, certification, safety, insurance, legal, approval, operating-condition, availability, or capture-outcome claims.
- Fake pilot profiles, fake galleries, fake reviews, fake credentials, fake guarantees, or unsupported project claims.
