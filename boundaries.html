Boundaries

Conservative language protects the customer and the work.

Higher Drones is presented as a project showcase media service. Drone-related capture remains review-dependent and should not be described as promised, cleared, credentialed, covered, or universally available.

Start with contextReview proof rules

Do not present as settled

  • Permission or approval status.
  • Location or site availability.
  • Credential, coverage, or operating-condition status.
  • Assured flight, capture, timing, or outcome.

Do say

  • Requests are reviewed before work starts.
  • Scope is confirmed manually.
  • Final package may use approved ground, exterior, elevated, detail, and before/after assets.
  • Customer details are published only with approval.

Review-dependent

The package starts with the project story, not a promise of flight.

That keeps the service useful even when a request is better served by non-aerial media, existing assets, or a narrower handoff.
