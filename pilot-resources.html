Portfolio presentation

  • Show category fit clearly.
  • Use permissioned visuals only.
  • Describe creative style in plain language.
  • Avoid unsupported credentials or outcome claims.

Project-fit notes

  • Best-fit project types.
  • Deliverable examples.
  • Planning questions clients should answer.
  • Boundaries that need review before scope.

Client communication

  • Keep first messages high-level.
  • Request only necessary context.
  • Avoid private details until needed.
  • Clarify what remains unconfirmed.

Marketplace posture

  • No guaranteed placement.
  • No creator paywall framing.
  • No side-revenue positioning.
  • No data-product positioning.

Creator standard

Lead with portfolio quality and clear fit.
