About Higher Drones

A Better Way to Book Aerial Content

Higher Drones is a drone videography marketplace that connects clients with skilled aerial pilots for professional photography, video, and custom aerial projects.

Find a Drone PilotExplore Aerial Work

Mission

Higher Drones exists to make professional aerial photography and videography easier to discover, compare, and book.

  • Portfolio discovery.
  • Service comparison.
  • Project request planning.
  • Aerial creator support.

What clients can do

Clients can explore aerial work, compare pilot profiles, review services, and prepare a project request.

  • Find drone pilots.
  • Explore aerial portfolios.
  • Compare packages.
  • Request custom projects.

What stays reviewed

Drone-related work requires careful review before scope or production assumptions are discussed.

  • Project fit.
  • Location context.
  • Timing and deliverables.
  • Permission and privacy considerations.

What the site avoids

The public site stays simple and client-facing.

  • No internal business-model language.
  • No complicated software dashboard.
  • No transaction system or automatic scheduling backend.
  • No approval, credential, coverage, or operating claims.

Next step
