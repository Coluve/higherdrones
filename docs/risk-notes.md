# Risk Notes — Higher Drones

## Risk level

High

## Primary risk notes

Drone operations require operator, safety, location, airspace, insurance, and legal compliance review before paid operations.

## Watch areas

- Payments
- Customer data
- Contact forms
- Legal/compliance claims
- Analytics/tracking
- Domain/DNS settings
- Regulated services
- Unsupported claims
- Insurance/licensing requirements
- Safety-sensitive services

## Rule

Pause for human approval before implementing R3 or R4 work.
